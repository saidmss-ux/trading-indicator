# TDSS v2 — Source of Truth

## 1. Purpose

TDSS v2 is a market observation and contextualization system.

TDSS v2 is not a signal generator, prediction engine, trade recommendation system, strategy engine, or automation framework. The trader remains the sole decision maker.

## 2. Core Principle

TDSS v2 may:

- observe
- classify
- score
- contextualize
- visualize
- explain

TDSS v2 may not:

- recommend trades
- predict future price
- infer trader intent
- suggest entries
- suggest exits
- suggest targets
- suggest stop losses
- suggest direction

Interpretation always belongs to the trader.

## 3. Market Philosophy

The market is viewed as a collection of participants holding or adjusting positions. Price is considered a consequence of participant behavior. TDSS v2 models observable participant activity areas rather than future price movement.

## 4. Structure Model

Market structure is represented by:

- HH
- HL
- LH
- LL

Structure describes how participants are organizing price. Structure is contextual and is not a trading signal.

## 5. Trend Context

Trend is a contextual force. It may influence how participant activity is interpreted, but it does not create decisions, recommendations, or directional outputs.

Accepted context labels include:

- bullish structure context
- bearish structure context
- neutral structure context

These labels describe observation only.

## 6. Zones

Support and Resistance zones are observational constructs. They represent areas of potential participant activity.

A zone may be:

- stronger or weaker
- more active or less active
- more or less significant from a market activity perspective

A zone may never be interpreted by TDSS v2 as:

- buy area
- sell area
- target area
- stop-loss area
- entry area
- exit area

## 7. Compression and Expansion

Compression represents reduced range or volatility and potential stored market pressure. Compression does not imply direction.

Expansion represents observed range expansion or release of stored pressure. Expansion is an observation, not a signal.

## 8. Scoring

Scores measure potential market activity significance only.

Scores do not measure:

- probability of profit
- trade quality
- expected success
- expected direction
- recommendation strength

The only question answered by a score is:

> How significant is this area from a market activity perspective?

## 9. Explainability

Every visual element must be explainable through:

- WHAT: what the element is
- WHY: why it exists
- IMPACT: how participant activity may be affected

## 10. Prohibited Outputs

TDSS v2 must never generate, display, infer, score, or suggest:

- BUY
- SELL
- LONG
- SHORT
- ENTRY
- EXIT
- trade setup
- trade opportunity
- expected direction
- probable direction
- target
- stop loss
- risk/reward recommendation
- high probability trade

## 11. Final Objective

TDSS v2 provides a behavioral map of market participation. It reveals where participant activity may be relevant. It does not decide.
